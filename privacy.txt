PRIVACY POLICY
EatDiscounted
Last Updated: May 7, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
EatDiscounted ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the EatDiscounted web application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

EatDiscounted is designed to be used without creating an account. We collect minimal information:

Automatically Collected:
• Hashed IP address: Your IP address is hashed using SHA-256 (one-way, irreversible) and truncated to 16 characters. We never store your actual IP address. This hash is used solely to associate community reports and favorites with your device.
• Hashed User-Agent: Combined with your hashed IP to create a per-device identifier for favorites.
• Search queries: Restaurant names you search for are logged anonymously (without any user identifier) to improve search suggestions and caching. No personal information is associated with search queries.

User-Provided (optional):
• Community reports: When you confirm a restaurant is available on a specific platform (e.g., "I found Carbone on Bilt"), we store the restaurant name, platform, and your reporter hash.
• Favorites: Restaurant names you choose to save.

We do NOT collect:
• Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
• Payment information
• Location data
• Browsing history or tracking data

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use collected information solely for the following purposes:
• Displaying community-reported platform matches to other users
• Saving your favorited restaurants for your convenience
• Rate limiting API requests to prevent abuse (using hashed IP, not stored)
• Improving search accuracy based on community-confirmed matches

4. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE
We do not sell, rent, or trade any user information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

• Brave Search API: When you search for a restaurant, your search query (restaurant name) is sent to Brave Search to check for platform presence. No personal identifiers are included.
• Upside API: Restaurant search queries are sent to Upside's offers API using a geographic bounding box. No personal data is included.
• Bilt Rewards API: We download Bilt's full public merchant catalog to match against your query. No personal data is sent.
• Rewards Network API: Restaurant name queries are sent to the AAdvantage Dining search API. No personal data is included.
• Blackbird: We parse Blackbird's public sitemap XML. No personal data is involved.
• Supabase: Community reports and favorites (with hashed, non-reversible identifiers) are stored in Supabase PostgreSQL.
• Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process

No external service receives any personal identifier — searches are performed using restaurant names only.

5. DATA SECURITY
We implement reasonable technical safeguards:
• IP addresses are hashed with SHA-256 before any storage — your actual IP is never persisted
• Rate limiting prevents abuse (5 searches per 60 seconds, 10 reports per 60 seconds)
• All data is transmitted over HTTPS
• Community reports have unique constraints to prevent duplicate spam
• All external API results are cached in memory (1-hour TTL) to minimize external API calls

6. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION
• Community reports and favorites are stored in Supabase PostgreSQL
• API response caches are stored in memory and on disk (JSON files) — these contain only restaurant/platform data, no user information
• Hashed identifiers are retained as long as the associated report or favorite exists
• You may request deletion of your reports and favorites (identified by your device hash)

7. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES
You have the following rights:
• Anonymity: EatDiscounted requires no account, no email, and no personal information to use
• Community Reports: Participation is voluntary — you can search without ever submitting a report
• Favorites: You can remove saved favorites at any time
• Deletion: You may request removal of community reports associated with your device hash

8. COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE
EatDiscounted does not use cookies, localStorage, or any client-side tracking mechanisms. All data is stored server-side with hashed identifiers.

9. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
Our application queries the following third-party services to check restaurant availability. Only restaurant names and geographic coordinates are sent — never personal data:
• Brave Search API: https://brave.com/privacy/browser
• Supabase (database): https://supabase.com/privacy
• Upside: https://www.upside.com/privacy-policy
• Bilt Rewards: https://www.biltrewards.com/privacy
• Rewards Network (AAdvantage Dining): https://www.rewardsnetwork.com/privacy-policy
• Blackbird: https://www.blackbird.xyz/privacy

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
EatDiscounted does not collect personal information from any user, including children. The service is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification since no personal data is collected.
